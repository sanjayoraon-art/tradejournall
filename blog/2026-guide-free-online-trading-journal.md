## How to Analyze Your Trade Data to Stop Losing Money

Data is useless without deep interpretation. Most amateurs glance at their gross green or red P&L number at 4:00 PM and shut down their screens. That is amateur behavior and a guaranteed path back to your 9-to-5 job.

### Dissecting Your Win Rate and Risk-Reward Framework
Your hit rate does not matter in isolation. A 90% win rate is absolute garbage if your 10% losers wipe out all your progressive gains. You must ruthlessly optimize your **risk-reward ratio**. Are you actually letting your winners run to a 1:3 payout, or are you choking them early at 1:1 out of sheer anxiety?

Using a professional **trade journal to improve winning rate** is about finding what setups carry the highest probability for *your* specific psychological profile. Filter your historical trades by 'Day of the Week' or 'Duration in Trade'. You might discover a brutal statistical reality: you print money in the opening 30 minutes of the session but give it all back fighting chop in the afternoon. Stop trading the afternoon. Boom—you just became profitable.
